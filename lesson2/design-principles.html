Design Principles

Jordan Postak

White Space

Apple

apple.com

The Apple website exemplifies the principle of white space by using large amounts of empty space around its products and text. This helps to create a sense of elegance and sophistication, and guides the viewer's eye towards the most important elements on the page.

Rule of Thirds

National Geographic

nationalgeographic.com

The National Geographic website exemplifies the principle of the Rule of Thirds by using a grid-based layout that divides the page into sections. This helps to create a sense of balance and symmetry, and draws the viewer's eye towards important elements such as images and headlines.

PARC: Proximity

Amazon

amazon.com

The Amazon website exemplifies the principle of proximity by grouping related elements together, such as product images, titles, and prices. This helps to create a sense of organization and hierarchy, and makes it easier for users to find what they're looking for.
