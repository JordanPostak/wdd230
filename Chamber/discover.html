Sandpoint Named One Of The Coolest in The US

When?: January 11th 2023

Where?: Matador Network

Sandpoint, Idaho has recently earned the prestigious recognition of being one of the coolest small towns in the country, according to Matador Network. The selection process involved capturing the essence of what makes a community unique and what ignites that magical small town vibe by listening to the stories of residents and travelers.

Sandpoint stood out due to its incredible outdoor adventure opportunities, its proximity to Silverwood, the Northwest's largest theme park, and its picturesque location nestled between three mountain ranges and the stunning Lake Pend Oreille. Despite its small size, Sandpoint surprises visitors with its thriving dining and arts scene, boasting a vibrant main street filled with local restaurants, small businesses, and quirky art galleries.

With a population of only 8,000 permanent residents, Sandpoint maintains a relaxed and laid-back atmosphere, allowing visitors to truly experience the charm of a close-knit community. The journey to Sandpoint is equally memorable, with scenic byways and mountain ranges adding to its natural beauty. This delightful Idaho town has previously been recognized as "the most beautiful town in America" by USA Today in 2012.

In summary, Sandpoint exemplifies a truly special community with an abundance of charm and captivating experiences to offer. It is a destination that combines outdoor adventures, proximity to a popular theme park, a thriving dining and arts scene, and a relaxed atmosphere that allows visitors to truly immerse themselves in the town's unique charm.
