Carlsbad :

A coastal city in northern San Diego County, California.

Population of approximately 115,000 residents.

Known for its beautiful beaches, Carlsbad is also home to popular attractions like LEGOLAND California, Carlsbad Village, and the Flower Fields.

While Carlsbad is primarily known for its coastal attractions, there are also local farms and agricultural businesses in the area. Some farms in the vicinity grow a variety of fruits and vegetables, including avocados, citrus fruits, and berries.

Huntington Beach :

A coastal city in Orange County, California.

Population of approximately 85,000 residents.

Newport Beach is renowned for its harbor, upscale residential areas, and scenic coastline. It offers opportunities for boating, water sports, shopping, and dining. Balboa Island and the Balboa Fun Zone are popular attractions.

While Newport Beach is known more for its recreational activities and luxury lifestyle, there are farms and agricultural areas in nearby regions of Orange County. Some farms focus on growing crops like avocados, oranges, and specialty fruits.
