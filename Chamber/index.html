Discover Sandpoint

Explore the endless possibilities in Sandpoint, Idaho. Experience the beauty, adventure, and vibrant community that awaits. Whether you're seeking outdoor thrills, cultural events, or delicious local cuisine, Sandpoint has something for everyone. Discover breathtaking landscapes, charming small-town atmosphere, and a welcoming community that will make you feel right at home.

Events

Sandpoint Farmer's Market

Date: July 15, 2023
Time: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
Location: Farmin Park, Sandpoint

Join us for the weekly Sandpoint Farmer's Market, where you can find a wide variety of fresh produce, local crafts, delicious food, and live music. Explore the vibrant community atmosphere and support local vendors.

Sandpoint Summer Festival

Date: July 22-24, 2023
Location: Downtown Sandpoint

Get ready for three days of non-stop fun at the Sandpoint Summer Festival. Enjoy live performances from local musicians, indulge in delicious food from various vendors, browse through unique arts and crafts, and participate in exciting activities for all ages. Don't miss the grand fireworks display on the final night!

News Releases

New Roller Coaster Coming to Silverwood Theme Park

Date: September 1, 2023

Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping experience as Silverwood Theme Park announces the opening of a thrilling new roller coaster. This exciting addition will provide riders with twists, turns, and heart-stopping drops. Don't miss out on the excitement and visit Silverwood Theme Park to experience this new coaster firsthand!

Sandpoint Named Best Small Town for Outdoor Recreation

Date: September 15, 2023

We are thrilled to announce that Sandpoint has been recognized as the "Best Small Town for Outdoor Recreation" by Outdoor Magazine. With its stunning natural beauty, access to hiking trails, water activities, and a vibrant community, Sandpoint offers endless opportunities for outdoor enthusiasts. Come and experience why Sandpoint is a haven for adventure seekers!
